Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often do I need to refill the water reservoir?

Typically, the reservoir needs refilling every two weeks, but this depends on environmental conditions.

2. Is the SMHG suitable for any type of plant?

SMHG is versatile and can support a wide variety of plants, particularly herbs and small vegetables such as basil, mint, tomatoes, and peppers. However, larger plants or root vegetables like carrots and potatoes are not suitable for this system.

3. Do I need to use special water or additives for my garden?

We recommend using distilled or filtered water to prevent mineral buildup, which can interfere with the system’s operation. Nutrient solutions are included with your initial purchase and refills can be bought from our website.

4. How much electricity does the system use?

The SMHG is designed to be energy efficient. It uses approximately the same amount of energy as a standard light bulb, making it economical to run 24/7.

5. How do I set up my SMHG after it arrives?

Setup is simple and straightforward. Detailed instructions are provided, and you can also access our setup video tutorials on our website. Typically, it takes less than an hour to get your garden up and running.
